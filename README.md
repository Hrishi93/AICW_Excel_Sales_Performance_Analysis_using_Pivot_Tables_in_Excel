## üß© Project Overview

This project demonstrates how to perform **Sales Performance Analysis** using **Microsoft Excel Pivot Tables** and **Pivot Charts**.
The objective is to analyze retail sales data to uncover insights on sales trends, top-performing products, regional performance, and key business metrics through interactive dashboards and visual reports.

---

## üéØ Problem Statement

A retail company wants to analyze its sales performance across **regions**, **products**, and **time periods** to identify trends, top-performing items, and areas for improvement.
Currently, the company has raw sales transaction data but lacks structured insights for decision-making.

As a **Data Analyst**, your tasks are to:

1. Summarize total sales and quantity sold.
2. Compare performance across different regions and products.
3. Identify top-performing region and product.
4. Track sales trends over time.
5. Provide key performance indicators (KPIs) for management.
6. Highlight year-over-year or month-over-month comparisons.
7. Build an interactive summary dashboard using Pivot Tables and Charts.

---

## üß† Objectives

* Transform raw data into meaningful insights.
* Visualize performance metrics by **Region, Product, and Time**.
* Use **Pivot Tables**, **Pivot Charts**, **Slicers**, and **Timelines** for analysis.
* Present findings through a **Dashboard** and **KPI Summary**.

---

## üìÇ Tasks Performed

### **Task 1: Total Sales & Quantity by Region**

**Goal:** Summarize total sales and quantity per region and show % of total sales.
**Steps:**

* Region ‚Üí Rows
* Sales ‚Üí Values (Sum)
* Quantity ‚Üí Values (Sum)
* Add "% of Grand Total" for Sales
* Format as Currency and Sort by Sales (Descending)

**Expected Output:**
East: 116,000 (36.36%) | West: 71,500 (22.41%) | South: 71,500 (22.41%) | North: 60,000 (18.81%)

---

### **Task 2: Region √ó Category Analysis (with Pivot Chart)**

**Goal:** Analyze sales by region and category with visual representation.
**Steps:**

* Region ‚Üí Rows
* Category ‚Üí Columns
* Sales ‚Üí Values
* Insert Column Pivot Chart

**Expected Output:**
Matrix of Sales by Region and Category with corresponding Pivot Chart.

---

### **Task 3: Top Products by Sales (Ranking & Highlighting)**

**Goal:** Identify top-selling products and visually highlight the best performers.
**Steps:**

* Product ‚Üí Rows
* Sales ‚Üí Values
* Sort Descending by Sales
* Add Rank (Show Values As ‚Üí Rank Largest to Smallest)
* Apply Conditional Formatting ‚Üí Top 3 Items

**Expected Output:**
Top 3 products highlighted (e.g., Laptop, TV, Mobile).

---

### **Task 4: Sales Trend by Month (Time-Series Analysis)**

**Goal:** Display sales trend over time using a line chart.
**Steps:**

* Date ‚Üí Rows
* Sales ‚Üí Values
* Group Dates by Months/Years
* Insert Line Chart and Add Data Labels

**Expected Output:**
Line chart showing monthly sales performance.

---

### **Task 5: Interactive Dashboard with Slicers & Timeline**

**Goal:** Create an interactive dashboard for dynamic data exploration.
**Steps:**

* Pivot Table: Region ‚Üí Rows, Category ‚Üí Columns, Sales ‚Üí Values
* Insert Pivot Chart
* Add Slicers (Region, Category)
* Add Timeline (Date)
* Arrange elements neatly into a single dashboard view

**Expected Output:**
Interactive dashboard to filter data by Region, Category, and Date.

---

### **Task 6: KPI Report (Key Performance Indicators)**

**Goal:** Summarize major business KPIs.
**KPIs Include:**

* Total Sales (Sum of Sales)
* Total Quantity (Sum of Quantity)
* Average Sales per Order
* Top Region by Sales
* Top Product by Sales

**Expected Output:**
KPI Summary (e.g., Total Sales = 319,000 | Top Region = East | Top Product = Laptop)
